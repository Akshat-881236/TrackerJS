Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Akshat Network Hub (ANH) processes authentication systems, analytics, diagnostics, navigation intelligence, feedback systems, and dashboard services across interconnected ANH platforms.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Akshat Network Hub (ANH) and all associated dashboards, projects, Firebase-powered services, tools, and websites hosted under official ANH infrastructure.

Official ANH domains generally follow:

https://akshat-881236.github.io/

2. Information We Collect

ANH now operates a Firebase-powered dashboard ecosystem with authenticated services, diagnostics infrastructure, navigation intelligence systems, and analytics support.

The platform may process:
  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth (DOB)
  • Profile Photo URL
  • Whatsapp Number
  • Visit History & Navigation Logs
  • Current, Previous & Next Page Relationships
  • Log IDs & Navigation Chains
  • Device, Browser & Viewport Metadata
  • Performance Diagnostics & Runtime Warnings
  • Feedback linked with Visit Logs
  • SEO Breadcrumb Metadata

3. How Information Is Used

Collected information may support:

  • Dashboard Personalization
  • Cross-site Authentication Continuity
  • Visit History Management
  • Navigation Intelligence Systems
  • Diagnostics & Troubleshooting
  • Feedback Verification
  • Performance Optimization
  • Security Monitoring
  • SEO Breadcrumb Infrastructure

ANH does not intentionally sell sensitive personal data or private analytics information to third parties.

4. Cookies, Sessions & Tracking

ANH may use browser storage, Firebase session handling, cookies, local storage systems, analytics metadata, and navigation tracking systems to improve platform continuity and dashboard experiences.

Tracking systems may process:
  • Page URLs & Titles
  • Previous & Next Page Relationships
  • Visit Log IDs
  • Navigation Chains
  • Device Diagnostics
  • Performance Timing
  • Session-linked Breadcrumb Metadata

5. Third-Party Services & Infrastructure

ANH may integrate trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Google Sign-In
  • Cloudflare Services
  • Open Source Libraries
  • PWA Runtime Systems

These services operate under their own policies and operational infrastructures.

6. User Rights & Controls

Authenticated users may:

  • Access dashboard profile data
  • Review visit history & diagnostics
  • Submit platform feedback
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of voluntarily submitted data
  • Manage browser-side storage where applicable

7. Children’s Privacy

ANH does not knowingly target or intentionally collect sensitive data from children under the legally required age.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect infrastructure upgrades, Firebase integrations, dashboard systems, analytics transitions, diagnostics engines, or legal requirements.

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related concerns, diagnostics issues, dashboard support, or policy inquiries, please contact ANH through official Network Hub channels.
